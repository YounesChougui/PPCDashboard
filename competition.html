Meet the Main Players in Georgia

Samsara

They use a powerful, all-in-one "Connected Operations Cloud" with slick AI features to help businesses boost their efficiency and get a clear return on their investment.

  • ✓ Pros: A single, smart platform for safety and efficiency.
  • ✓ How They Sell: A flexible monthly subscription that's very competitive on price.
  • ✓ Cons: Their all-in-one platform can feel complex, and their large scale can seem impersonal.

Verizon Connect

Verizon Connect is the trusted household name. They lean on the powerful Verizon brand to offer a premium, super-reliable service, often locking in big companies with long contracts.

  • ✓ Pros: A reliable and secure solution with all the bells and whistles.
  • ✓ How They Sell: Higher-priced, service-based plans, usually with a three-year commitment.
  • ✓ Cons: Those long, pricey contracts can be a major turn-off for smaller and growing businesses.

How They Get Your Attention

Both Samsara and Verizon Connect have huge marketing budgets and aren't afraid to use them. They're all over Google and LinkedIn, making it tough to compete head-on.
